Russian salted salmon recipe

Back to main page

Description

A recipe for salted salmon I grabbed off MelanieCooks.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound salmon fillet
  • 2 tablespoon salt
  • 2 teaspoon sugar

Instructions

  1. If the salmon filet is thicker than 1/2 inch, cut it into 2 layers (so each layer is less than 1/2 inch thick).

  2. Put the salmon filet in the dish. Rub the salt and sugar into both sides of the salmon.

  3. Close the dish with the salmon and put in the refrigerator.

  4. Leave salted salmon in the refrigerator for 4 days. After 4 days, slice it into thin slices and serve.
